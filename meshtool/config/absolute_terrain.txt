# ROCK-ICE/ROCK (ABSOLUTE PER TEMPERATURE)
#
# ROCK-ICE/ROCK are just the closest environment to glacier but warmer temp, they are mainly rocks with few ""snow tongues"".
# ROCK-ICE/ROCK will cover any cold terrain with any slope before cliff, BUT  if the temp range is higher than 35 deg then polar desert will be replaced by tundra-grass and snow appears only if temperature is colder than -16 deg
#
# ROCK-ICE/ROCK have different rules for slope heading vs temperature, so that southern slope will have snow tongues with colder temperatures (less snow) and northern slopes will have snow tongues with warmer temperature (more snow)
#
